Q1) Ans: Society impacts us as individuals through our values, perceptions, languages, and beliefs. These differences often hinder communication between people from different backgrounds and can cause miscommunication, misunderstandings, or conflicts. Since our social perception shapes how we interpret others’ messages, recognizing and respecting cultural and perceptual differences is essential. By considering others’ perspectives and avoiding stereotypes, we can reduce communication barriers and achieve more effective interaction.

Q2) Ans: Employees coming from different backgrounds and culture can hinder the process of communication which can cause misunderstandings, conflicts, biasness or discrimination. Thus embracing and understanding each other's cultural background facilitates cross-cultural communication in a workspace environment.
